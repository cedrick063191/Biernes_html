About Our Resort

At DAYA Farm Adventure, we believe in promoting sustainable agriculture and fostering connections between people and the land. Our farm is a haven where families, friends, and individuals can explore organic farming practices, indulge in farm-to-table dining experiences, and engage in a variety of outdoor activities.

DAYA Farm Adventure is a unique agritourism destination nestled in the heart of lush countryside landscapes. Offering a refreshing escape from urban life, DAYA Farm Adventure invites visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty of nature while experiencing the joys of rural living.
